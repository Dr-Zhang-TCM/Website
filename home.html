What is acupuncture?

It's a traditional Chinese medicine technique that involves inserting thin needles into specific points on the body to treat various health conditions and symptoms.

What is cupping?

It's another medicine technique where glass cups are used to create suction on the skin, typically to relieve pain or improve circulation
